WIN & ARCHERY

We love archery!

We are a private archery club based in Chantilly, VA. The club was founded in 2016 by Coach Dong Won Kang to introduce recurve archery to the next generation of archers with hopes of competing internationally.

In just under three years, our growing Olympic Recurve Archery Team has trained tirelessly to bring home more than 75 medals from local, state, and national competitions, all while breaking multiple established records.

We are extremely proud of our dedicated young archers who continue to grow and push themselves as individual athletes and as respectful members of the broader global archery community. Creative Commons license.

Archery Teaches Goal Setting

In archery, the focus is on the target. Archery offers a great goal-setting environment. Starting even with simple goals, like focusing on smaller target rings and increasing distance, young archers can set personal goals for each practice session and monitor their progress as they develop into the archers they want to become.

Archery Gives a Sense of Accomplishment

Not all kids like team sports. Individual sports such as archery may give these kids a place to shine. Being in a position to pull back the string and loose an arrow into the target will surely give a child a great sense of accomplishment.

Archery is a Social Sport

Individuals from all walks of life can enjoy archery. Every age group and ability level practice and compete at events. It is not uncommon to see 50 year olds alongside 14 year olds giving tips and advice on the range. Even on the competition field, archers of all ages and abilities gather to pit their skills against one another all while helping each other grow.
