MEET OUR COACHES

Coach Kang

With more than 40 years of archery experience and 133 international medals, Coach Dong Won Kang is dedicated to helping our archers reach their full potential. Win & Archery’s coach, Dong Won Kang picked up a bow and arrow for the first time when he was only 10-years old. Our coach is no stranger to overcoming the obstacles that come with becoming a great archer.

In just under three years, our growing Olympic Recurve Archery Team has trained tirelessly to bring home more than 75 medals from local, state, and national competitions, all while breaking multiple established records.

We are extremely proud of our dedicated young archers who continue to grow and push themselves as individual athletes and as respectful members of the broader global archery community.
